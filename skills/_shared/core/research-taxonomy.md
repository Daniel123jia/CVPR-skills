# Research Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy when generating conference reports, paper notes, and idea cards. Assign multiple labels when useful; do not force a single category.

## Vision Tasks

- recognition and classification
- detection and localization
- segmentation
- tracking and motion
- 3D vision and geometry
- generation and editing
- multimodal vision-language
- embodied vision and robotics
- medical and scientific imaging
- remote sensing and geospatial vision

## Method Families

- representation learning
- foundation models
- diffusion and generative models
- transformers and sequence models
- self-supervised and weakly supervised learning
- efficient inference and compression
- data-centric learning
- robustness and safety
- evaluation and benchmarking
- systems and deployment

## Research Reading Axes

- problem setting
- key technical idea
- data and supervision
- evaluation protocol
- empirical result
- limitation
- reproducibility signal
- follow-up opportunity

## Idea Generation Axes

- combine two method families
- transfer a method to a neglected task
- improve evaluation or robustness
- reduce data, compute, or annotation cost
- expose a failure mode
- build a benchmark or dataset
- turn an observation into a tool
