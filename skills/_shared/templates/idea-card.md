# Idea Card Template

## Title

Short research idea title.

## Inspiration

Which CVPR papers, topics, or limitations inspired this idea?

## Hypothesis

State the testable claim.

## Proposed Method

Describe the method at a level that a researcher could start scoping.

## Evaluation

Datasets, baselines, metrics, and ablations.

## Risk

What could make the idea fail?

## First Experiment

The smallest experiment that would produce useful evidence.
