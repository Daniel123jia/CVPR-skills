# Expected Route: Title-Only Idea Warning

- Skill: `cvpr-idea-miner`
- Workflow: `topic-map`
- Evidence level: `title_only`
- Status: `preliminary` or `evidence_insufficient`

Expected behavior:

- Produce only title-level preliminary direction scanning.
- Explicitly warn that only titles are available.
- 不得输出方法细节、网络结构、数据集、baseline、实验结果、ablation、leaderboard、代码链接、项目主页或引用量。
- Do not provide expected experimental results.
- Ask for abstracts, reader notes, or fulltext-derived notes before deeper gap analysis, method recombination, idea cards, or experiment plans.
